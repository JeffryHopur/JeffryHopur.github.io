Portable 3D Printer

Portable 3D printer

This project is based on the Positron V3 model printer with some improvements. The heating bed is made out of ITO glass to allow for a see-through effect. The printer prints upside down to allow for weight reduction on the base.

Since it prints upside down the system has a lower center of gravity allowing us to use a lighter base. The nozzle and the bed connections have also been improved
